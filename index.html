Handy Margin/Padding Classes

m-* and p-* classes allow you to add margin or padding to any element based on the rem unit of size. These sizes range from 1 to 5, 1 being the least amoutn of spacing.

  • m — all margins
  • mt — margin-top
  • mr — margin-right
  • ml — margin-left
  • mx — margin-left and margin-right
  • mb — margin-bottom
  • my — margin-top and margin-bottom

For example: m-1 give the element 1rem of margin all away around. mb-3 gives the element a margin-bottom of 3rems

  • p — all paddings
  • pt — padding-top
  • pr — padding-right
  • pl — padding-left
  • px — padding-left and padding-right
  • pb — padding-bottom
  • py — padding-top and padding-bottom
